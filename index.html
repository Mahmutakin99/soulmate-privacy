SoulMate ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the SoulMate mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and user ID.
  • Profile information you provide in the app.
  • Messages, media, and other content you choose to send or store through the app.
  • Relationship or pairing data needed to connect you with your partner.
  • Location data, only if you enable location-sharing features.
  • Notification-related data, such as push notification tokens.
  • Diagnostic and crash data to help us maintain app stability and fix issues.
  • Local preferences and app settings stored on your device.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the app’s core features.
  • Authenticate users and keep accounts secure.
  • Sync messages, media, and relationship-related data.
  • Enable optional features such as location sharing, widgets, and live activities.
  • Send push notifications and important service updates.
  • Monitor performance, diagnose crashes, and improve reliability.

3. Photos, Media, and Device Access

SoulMate may allow you to select images or videos from your device using Apple system pickers. We only access content that you explicitly choose.

If you choose to save photos or videos to your Photos app, the app may request permission to add content to your photo library.

If you enable location sharing, the app may request location access while you use the app.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information in the following cases:

  • With your linked partner, when sharing is part of the app’s functionality.
  • With service providers that help us operate the app, such as Firebase services.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the app.

5. Third-Party Services

SoulMate may use third-party services such as:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Firebase Cloud Functions
  • Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services may process data as needed to provide their functionality.

6. Data Storage and Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Some settings and cached content may also be stored locally on your device until you remove them or uninstall the app.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Your Choices

You may be able to:

  • Update certain profile information in the app.
  • Disable optional permissions such as location or notifications in device settings.
  • Request account deletion through in-app functionality or by contacting us.

9. Children's Privacy

SoulMate is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Users

If you use the app outside your home country, your information may be processed and stored in countries where our service providers operate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.
